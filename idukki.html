  IDUKKI





     Telephone Access Code: Thekkady, Kumily, Peermedu: +91 4869; Munnar: +91 4865; Chinnakanal: +91 4868
     
     Idukki is Kerala's second largest district and has been blessed with numerous natural wonders. The high valleys
     and hill ranges of this landlocked region are fed by three major rivers - Periyar, Thalayar and Thodupuzhayar and
     their tributaries. Its forest still hosts many tribes and over half of the terrain is still covered with forests. People love
     going to the gigantic arch dam for getting its mesmerising view.
