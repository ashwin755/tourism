  PALAKAD





     Telephone Access Code: +91 491
     
     Celebrated as the Granary of Kerala, Palakkad is a vast stretch of fertile plains interspersed with hills, rivers,
     mountain streams and forests. The gateway to Kerala from the north, a 40 kilometre break in the mountains
     known as the Palakkad Gap gives access to this land situated at the foot of the Western Ghats. The pass acts
     as a corridor between Kerala and the neighbouring Tamil Nadu and played a key role in the trade relations between the east
     and west coasts of peninsular India.
